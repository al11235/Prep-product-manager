# STAR Method — Behavioral Questions

Use this for "Tell me about a time when..." questions.

## The Framework

| Component | What to Cover | Time |
|-----------|--------------|------|
| **S** — Situation | Context. Where, when, what was happening. Keep brief. | ~15 sec |
| **T** — Task | Your specific responsibility or challenge. What was at stake? | ~15 sec |
| **A** — Action | What YOU did. Be specific. Use "I" not "we." | ~60 sec |
| **R** — Result | Quantified outcome. What you learned. Business impact. | ~30 sec |

## Total target: ~2 minutes per story

## Story Bank Template

Build 8–10 stories that cover these themes:

| Theme | Maps to (Amazon LPs) | Example Prompt |
|-------|----------------------|----------------|
| Ambiguity / incomplete data | Bias for Action, Dive Deep | "Time you made a decision without complete info" |
| Conflict / disagreement | Have Backbone, Earn Trust | "Time you disagreed with your manager" |
| Failure / mistake | Learn and Be Curious, Ownership | "Time you failed and what you learned" |
| Influence without authority | Earn Trust, Have Backbone | "Time you convinced a team to change direction" |
| Customer obsession | Customer Obsession | "Time you went above and beyond for a customer" |
| Delivering under pressure | Deliver Results, Bias for Action | "Time you delivered on a tight deadline" |
| Innovation | Invent and Simplify | "Time you found a creative solution" |
| Data-driven decision | Dive Deep | "Time you used data to influence a decision" |

## Tips
- Prepare stories from your BIE work — data pipelines, dashboards, stakeholder influence
- Quantify results: "reduced query time by 40%", "influenced $2M roadmap decision"
- Show cross-functional impact — you worked with PMs, engineers, leadership
- For PM interviews, emphasize: customer thinking, prioritization, influence, ownership
- Practice out loud — 2 minutes feels shorter than you think

## Anti-Patterns to Avoid
- ❌ Too much situation, not enough action
- ❌ Using "we" throughout — interviewer wants YOUR contribution
- ❌ No measurable result
- ❌ Story doesn't match the question asked
- ❌ Rambling past 3 minutes
