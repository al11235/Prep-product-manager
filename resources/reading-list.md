# Reading List & Resources — Product Data Scientist

## Books (Priority Order)

### Must-Read
1. **Trustworthy Online Controlled Experiments** by Kohavi, Tang, Xu — ✅ You have this. Core experimentation bible.
2. **Naked Statistics** by Charles Wheelan — Intuition-building for stats concepts (quick read)
3. **Causal Inference: The Mixtape** by Scott Cunningham — Free online. Causal inference methods.

### Recommended
4. **Thinking, Fast and Slow** by Daniel Kahneman — Behavioral biases, useful for product sense
5. **The Book of Why** by Judea Pearl — Causal reasoning (conceptual, not heavy math)
6. **Python for Data Analysis** by Wes McKinney — pandas reference (skim, not cover-to-cover)

### Optional / Deep Dives
7. **Statistical Rethinking** by Richard McElreath — Bayesian thinking (if you want depth)
8. **Designing Data-Intensive Applications** by Martin Kleppmann — System understanding (your BIE background helps here)
