# Behavioral Questions

Use STAR method. Target 2 minutes per answer. Prepare 8–10 stories that flex across themes.

## Leadership & Influence
1. Tell me about a time you led a project without formal authority.
2. Describe a time you convinced a skeptical stakeholder to support your idea.
3. Tell me about a time you had to influence a team's direction using data.

## Conflict & Disagreement
4. Tell me about a time you disagreed with your manager. What happened?
5. Describe a situation where two teams had conflicting priorities. How did you handle it?
6. Tell me about a time you received critical feedback. How did you respond?

## Ambiguity & Decision-Making
7. Tell me about a time you had to make a decision with incomplete information.
8. Describe a project where requirements kept changing. How did you manage?
9. Tell me about a time you had to define success criteria from scratch.

## Failure & Learning
10. Tell me about your biggest professional failure. What did you learn?
11. Describe a time a project didn't go as planned. What would you do differently?
12. Tell me about a time you took a risk that didn't pay off.

## Customer Focus
13. Tell me about a time you went above and beyond for a customer or end user.
14. Describe a time you used customer feedback to change a product decision.

## Delivering Results
15. Tell me about a time you delivered something under a tight deadline.
16. Describe your most impactful project. What made it successful?
